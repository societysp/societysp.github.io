The Society for Structuralist Philosophy seeks to create a space of critical inquiry into questions regarding form, thought, and discourse in the arts, humanities, and social sciences. It seeks to explore the borders and limits of conceptuality and figuration. It takes inspiration from the developments of modern philosophy, psychoanalysis, and textual criticism.
