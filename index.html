The Society for Structuralist Philosophy (SSP) focuses on the dissemination, preservation, and fostering of inquiry into areas of study associated with modern philosophy, intellectual history, theoretical psychoanalysis, and critical theory. The organization seeks to achieve these ends through the formation of a publishing house, lecture series, and other educational and scholarly initiatives.

We seek to welcome scholars from a wide range of discplinary backgrounds: semiotics, philosophy of lanugage, philosophy of mathematics, philosophy of science, sociology of knowledge, literary theory, intellectual history, historiography, art history, theoretical psychoanalysis, systems theory, science and technology studies, anthropology, information studies, and any others with an interest in the notion of structure and structuralism.

The SSP's goal can be stated simply: a re-turn to structure.
